My story

I'm a young woman in her early 20s with a burning passion for technology and a dream of becoming a developer. My journey into the world of coding began with a curiosity that quickly evolved into a deep fascination with the endless possibilities that programming offers.

From an early age, I found myself captivated by the transformative power of technology. As I delved into the realms of programming languages and web development, I discovered the joy of translating ideas into lines of code.

The dream of becoming a developer is not just about mastering programming languages; it's about contributing to a future where technology is a force for positive change. I envision myself creating applications that make a difference, whether by simplifying daily tasks or by sparking moments of joy in users' lives.

As a young woman in tech, I am not just pursuing a career; I am shaping a narrative, and I am excited to see where this journey will take me.

Here's to coding, learning, and building a future where anything is possible!
